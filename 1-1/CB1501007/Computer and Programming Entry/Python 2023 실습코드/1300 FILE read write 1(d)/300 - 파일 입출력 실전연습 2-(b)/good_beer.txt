Deputy PM Numan Kurtulmus said the Dutch ambassador would be 
barred from returning to Ankara, and high-level political 
discussions suspended.

Turkish attempts to hold rallies in Germany, Austria, 
Switzerland and the Netherlands have been blocked.

President Recep Tayyip Erdogan accused the Dutch 
and Germans of Nazism.
The BBC's Mark Lowen in Istanbul says Turkey and the Netherlands, 
two Nato allies, are now locked in an unprecedented diplomatic crisis.
